AI's Positive Contribution to Society

Image obtained from https://miappi.com/incorporating-ai-in-digital-marketing/

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has repidly become one of the most discussed in computing. Most of this popularity can be attruibuted to the surge in the popularity of Neural Networks, a computational model that tries to mimic the brain and make decisions similarly to humans. In just under 60 years, Neural Networks are now capable of AI so complex that solving a game as intricate as GO is now possible.

Nevertheless, Neural Networks' (and by extension AI's) usage is not limited to only games. For instance, Tesla has been refining their Neural Network technology so that it can eventually be used in self-driving cars.

Additionally, AI has also been used to facilitate our lives. For example, the Uber app remembers what locations you've traveled to from certain places so that next time the process is faster. It's also very prevalent in social media, where advertisements are targeted towards your search history, or what posts you've interacted with.

AI's contribution to society has been nothing short of outstanding, and in the future this will only become more apparent with it being incorporated into all parts of our lives.
